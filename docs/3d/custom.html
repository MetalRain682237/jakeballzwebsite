Custom made doesn't mean I have to make it. If you have a file that you made or something. I can probably use it. One thing I will not do though is make something off the internet and sell it to you. Most of the time this is a copyright issue.

Custom-made models will very greatly in price. Here is what I will typically charge:

Design time: $12/hour
- time I spend designing a model for you

Filament: $0.06/gram
- depending on size, that can be typically like 10g ($0.60) to 100g ($6.00)

Print time: $1/hour
- time it takes for the model to print, time will be rounded up. Typically like 2+ hours for anything of decent size

Example: An item that took 30 minutes to design, used 25g of filament and took 3 hours to print would cost $10.5
